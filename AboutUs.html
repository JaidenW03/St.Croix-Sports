About Us

Meet our owner and our team of hockey enthusiasts.

Meet The Owner

Sean Coffey, a passionate hockey enthusiast committed to providing the best gear and advice. Sean is the heart and soul of our store, bringing decades of experience and a deep love for the game to our community.

Our Team

Our team, a diverse group of hockey enthusiasts and experts, is united by a shared passion for the sport. From seasoned coaches to former players, each member brings unique skills and insights. Together, we strive to provide the best service, advice, and products to our community, ensuring every customer leaves our store ready to hit the ice with confidence.

Our Mission

Founded in May 2014, St. Croix Sports is western Wisconsin's leading hockey destination, located at 1830 Webster Street in Hudson, near the Hudson Sports and Civic Center. We specialize in offering a wide range of hockey equipment, personalized fittings, and expert advice to enhance the hockey experience for players and fans alike. Our mission is to ensure every customer has access to the best gear and knowledge to excel on the ice.
