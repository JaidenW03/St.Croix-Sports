What We Do

Founded in May 2014, St. Croix Sports is western Wisconsin's leading hockey destination, located at 1830 Webster Street in Hudson, near the Hudson Sports and Civic Center. We specialize in offering a wide range of hockey equipment, personalized fittings, and expert advice to enhance the hockey experience for players and fans alike. Our mission is to ensure every customer has access to the best gear and knowledge to excel on the ice.
